Welcome Aboard Captain!

Thank you for playing Make it So.
A couple of things you should know before you begin:

1. This game uses a cookie to associate your captain's profile with your browser. You do not have to register or create a profile before you can start playing and all of your actions will be saved automatically. In the future, you will be able to connect your profile to a social-media account and then log in and play from other browsers (but I haven't gotten to that yet).

If you would like to start over, you can delete your browser cookie. WARNING: Deleting your cookie will delete your captain profile, all of your ships, and all of your progress.

2. This game is in the super-early-alpha-more-like-proof-of-concept stage. Please do not expect a complete and bug-free game. I'll make a bug-reporter later, but if you want to tell me about something that went wrong, you can email me at hiscoresentertainment@gmail.com.

3. Actually, now that I think about it, if you have any questions or want to know more you can email me at hiscoresentertainment@gmail.com or do the twitter thing (which I am bad at).

4. Browser check! How could I forget about the browser check? Most modern, up-to-date browsers should work. CSS transitions and transforms are the only issue.
